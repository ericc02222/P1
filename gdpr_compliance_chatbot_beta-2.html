Research Context & Methodology

Research Problem

Gap between abstract GDPR principles and practical cloud implementation guidance for financial institutions, leading to compliance challenges and increased manual effort.

Proposed Solution

AI-powered model integrating knowledge representation, conversational guidance, and configuration analysis to translate GDPR requirements into actionable cloud guidance.

Evaluation Methodology

Comparative analysis against traditional compliance documentation, measuring improvements in accuracy, relevance, and detection capabilities.

Expected Contributions

Novel framework for GDPR-cloud mapping, innovative conversational AI approach, and methodology for automated compliance verification in financial services.
